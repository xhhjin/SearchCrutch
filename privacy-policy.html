Search Crutch Privacy Policy

"Search Crutch" is a search engine switching plug-in for WebExtensions browser, used to improve the efficiency of switching between different search engines when searching the Internet. By default, Search Crutch only downloads synced settings during the first installation, and if no synced settings are found, it will never sync with your browser account.

Privacy Policy

  • This plugin will not forcefully hijack access or insert malicious code under any circumstances.
  • This plug-in does not collect and store any device information, location information and personal identification information.
  • This plugin will not retain any of your information, and will not share any of your personal data with anyone.
  • This plug-in will identify the configured search website and analyze the search keywords when searching on the corresponding website to facilitate switching searches.
  • This plug-in will back up your custom settings through your browser account to improve user experience; if you don’t want to synchronize, uncheck "Automatically synchronize backup data" on the settings page.

Declaration

This privacy policy is an integral part of this plug-in service usage agreement.


搜索拐杖插件隐私保护政策

"搜索拐杖" 是一款适用于 WebExtensions 浏览器的搜索引擎切换插件，用于提升网络搜索时不同搜索引擎间的切换效率。

隐私相关

  • 本插件不会在任何情况下强行劫持访问、插入恶意代码。
  • 本插件不会收集和储存任何设备信息、位置信息和个人识别信息。
  • 本插件不会保留您的任何信息，不会与任何人共享您的任何个人数据。
  • 本插件会识别配置的搜索网站并在对应网站搜索时分析搜索关键词便于切换搜索。
  • 本插件会通过浏览器账户备份你的自定义设置，改善用户体验；如不愿意同步，在设置页面取消勾选“备份数据自动同步”即可。
